Praying App

Features:
•	Users are asked their prayer goals
•	Users can choose between Beginner, Intermediate and Pro
•	Beginner focuses on the user aiming to pray 1-5 prays
•	Intermediate focuses on the user aiming to pray all prays plus some addtionary ones
•	Pro focuses all the prayers including additional ones
•	User will achieve badges as they progress eg. 1 week streak
•	Users can view instructions on how to pray and perform wudhu
•	Users can view instructions on the different prayer types eg. fard, sunnah etc.
•	Kabha direction compass
